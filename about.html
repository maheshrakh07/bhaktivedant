Our Story

More Than Just a Hospital,
We Are a Family.

It all started in 1995 with a single belief: Healthcare should be compassionate, accessible, and world-class.

BhaktiVedant Hospital began as a modest 20-bed clinic founded by Dr. Rajesh Verma. Over the decades, driven by the trust of our community, we have transformed into a 500-bed quaternary care facility.

We don't just treat diseases; we treat people. Our walls have witnessed thousands of recovery stories, the first cries of newborns, and the smiles of families reunited. Today, we stand as a beacon of hope, equipped with futuristic technology but rooted in old-school values of care.

Our Journey

Milestones of Excellence

1995

The Inception

Founded as a small clinic with 20 beds and 5 doctors dedicated to General Medicine.

2005

First Expansion

Expanded to 100 beds. Inaugurated the dedicated Orthopedics and Cardiology wings.

2012

Critical Care Unit

Launched a 50-bed ICU and 24/7 Trauma Center with state-of-the-art ventilators.

2018

Digital Revolution

Implemented fully paperless records (EMR) and installed advanced MRI & CT Scanners.

2025

Global Recognition

Accredited by JCI. Now serving 500+ patients daily with a team of 150+ specialists.
