Hospital Facilities

World-Class Infrastructure for Advanced Care

Critical Care

Modern ICU & Operation Theatres

Our hospital is equipped with a state-of-the-art Intensive Care Unit (ICU) featuring advanced ventilators and 24/7 monitoring systems. Our Modular Operation Theatres (OT) are designed to maintain absolute sterility for complex surgeries, ensuring patient safety at all times.

  • Advanced Ventilators
  • Multipara Monitors
  • Laminar Air Flow OT
Diagnostics

Digital X-Ray & Pathology Lab

We provide accurate and quick diagnostic services under one roof. Our facility includes Digital X-Ray for high-resolution imaging and a fully automated Pathology Laboratory available 24 hours a day for blood tests and other investigations.

  • High-Res Digital X-Ray
  • Automated Blood Analyzer
  • 24-Hour Report Availability
Patient Comfort

Separate Wards & Private Rooms

Patient comfort and hygiene are our top priorities. We offer Separate General Wards for Men and Women to ensure privacy. For patients requiring isolation or extra comfort, we have deluxe private rooms with attached amenities.

  • Male & Female Wards
  • AC Private Rooms
  • High Standards of Hygiene
